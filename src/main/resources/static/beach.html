Slow Days, Salty Hair & Golden Sunsets

Explore India’s most beautiful coasts – from Goa to Gokarna, Varkala to the Andamans. Pick your vibe: party shores, quiet coves, or family-friendly beaches.

Top Beach Escapes

Choose your coastline – party-heavy, laid-back, or blissfully offbeat.

Goa

Baga · Calangute · Palolem

Legendary shacks, sunset parties, water sports and café culture. North Goa for nightlife, South Goa for quieter, scenic beaches.

  • #Nightlife
  • #WaterSports
  • #Cafés

Gokarna

Kudle · Om · Half Moon

Cliffside cafés, peaceful shores and short hikes between coves – perfect if you want Goan vibes without the big crowds.

  • #Chill
  • #Backpacker
  • #Sunsets

Varkala

Kerala · Cliffside Beach Town

Dramatic cliffs overlooking the Arabian Sea, Ayurvedic retreats and relaxed beach cafés – ideal for slow travel.

  • #CliffViews
  • #Ayurveda
  • #SlowTravel

Andaman & Nicobar Islands

Havelock · Neil Island

White sand, turquoise water, coral reefs and starry skies. Great for scuba, snorkeling and a more exclusive island feel.

  • #Scuba
  • #Snorkeling
  • #IslandLife

Your Perfect Beach Day

Use this as a mini blueprint, then jump back to the main planner to turn it into a full trip.

Morning

Sunrise walk, light breakfast at a shack and maybe a calm swim before crowds arrive.

  • Sunrise at the quietest stretch of beach
  • Local breakfast: poha, dosa or fresh fruit bowls
  • Short wander through nearby markets

Afternoon

Water activities and lazy hours under a beach umbrella with a book or playlist.

  • Water sports (parasailing, surfing, kayaking – depending on the coast)
  • Lunch at a sea-facing café
  • Midday siesta or spa / Ayurvedic massage

Sunset & Night

Switch from chill to party mode — or keep it slow with live music and bonfires.

  • Sunset viewpoint with camera in hand
  • Live music / bonfire / night market stroll
  • Stargazing or a quiet walk along the shore
